type 1
    anaphylactic
    IgE
    exogenous antigen
    15 minutes
    weal and flare
    examples
        hay fever
        allergic rhinitis

type 2
    cytotoxic
    IgG
    IgM
    cell surface antigen
    lysis and necrosis
    minutes-hours
    examples
        erythroblastosis fetalis
        Goodpasture nephritis

type 3
    immune complex
    IgG
    IgM
    soluble antigen
    erythema and edema
    3–8 hours
    examples
        lupus
        farmer's lung disease

type 4
    delayed
    no antibodies
        T-cell mediated
    tissues and organs act as antigen
    2–3 days
    erythema and induration
    examples
        PPD
        poison ivy
